Donor Agencies for Germany Scholarships

  • DAAD (Deutscher Akademischer Austauschdienst)

    The DAAD is the German Academic Exchange Service and is a publicly-funded independent organization of higher education institutions in Germany. It promotes international academic relations and cooperation by offering scholarship opportunities to international students and researchers.

  • Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung

    The Konrad Adenauer Foundation is a political foundation in Germany associated with the Christian Democratic Union. It offers scholarships to international students who have demonstrated academic excellence and commitment to social and political issues.

  • Friedrich-Ebert-Stiftung

    The Friedrich Ebert Foundation is a political foundation associated with the Social Democratic Party in Germany. It promotes political education and offers scholarships to international students who are committed to social justice and democracy.

  • Heinrich-Böll-Stiftung

    The Heinrich Böll Foundation is a political foundation associated with the Green Party in Germany. It promotes democracy, human rights, and environmental sustainability and offers scholarships to international students who share its values.

  • Rosa-Luxemburg-Stiftung

    The Rosa Luxemburg Foundation is a political foundation associated with the Left Party in Germany. It promotes democratic socialism and offers scholarships to international students who are committed to social and economic justice.
